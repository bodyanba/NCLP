Introduction

The National Collaborative Leadership Program (NCLP) is a high impact program focused on building an integrated community of public-private collaborative leaders. These leaders will be equipped with the skills, capabilities, and mindset required to accelerate the economic potential of Oman, promote its sustainable growth, and enhance its international competitiveness.

This innovative new program is the latest addition to the portfolio of National Programs of the Diwan of Royal Court, Sultanate of Oman, building on the success of its two existing initiatives for senior private and public sector leaders - the National CEO Program (NCP) and the National Leadership & Competitiveness Program (NLCP).

H.E. Dr Ali Qassim Jawad Al-Lawati
Advisor for Studies and Research to the Diwan of Royal Court

Welcome

It is my great pleasure to introduce you to the National Collaborative Leadership Program (NCLP). The most recent addition to the portfolio of National Programs of the Diwan of Royal Court, Sultanate of Oman, the NCLP builds on the success of its two existing initiatives for private and public sector leaders - the National CEO Program (NCP) and the National Leadership & Competitiveness Program (NLCP).

Bringing together talented senior individuals from Oman’s public and private sectors, the NCLP aims to develop a community of collaborative leaders with a shared commitment to accelerating the Sultanate’s economic progress and enhancing its international competitiveness.

In order to ensure NCLP participants are empowered with the skills, capabilities and mindset necessary for collaborative leadership, the National Programs Team has designed the NCLP learning journey in partnership with IMD Business School (Switzerland), Henley Business School (UK) and Nanyang Technological University (Singapore), among the most respected international organisations for leadership learning. Together, they have created an innovative program architecture that encompasses leading edge learning methodologies, draws on expert insight from world-class faculty and pre-eminent practitioners, and is delivered in both Oman and international locations.

I encourage forward-looking and ambitious Omani public and private sector leaders to apply for admission to the NCLP 2018 - 2019 Cohort and become part of the community. I greatly look forward to welcoming NCLP graduates to the National Programs alumni network and to advancing the economic potential of the Sultanate.

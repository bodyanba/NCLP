NCLP Community

The NCLP has been developed by the National Leadership Programs Team and is sponsored by the Diwan of Royal Court. The National Programs Team works in partnership with a community of high profile expert partners, each of whom is experienced in the design and delivery of learning in the Sultanate of Oman.

Sharaka

An extension of the patronage of His Majesty the Sultan for the private sector, Sharaka was launched in April 2013 to enable the sector to play its role as an active partner in the social and economic development of the Sultanate.

Prioritising economic diversification, job creation, regional development and the building of national capabilities, the objectives of this public-private partnership include:

01

partnership

Enhancing co-operation between the public and private sector

02

collaboration

Identifying opportunities for collaboration

03

Supporting

Supporting the employment and capability development of Omani nationals

04

development

Working towards regional diversification and development.

The National Programs is just one of a number of key national initiatives that Sharaka is involved in or has under development.

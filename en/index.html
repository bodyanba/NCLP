About The Program

The National Collaborative Leadership Program (NCLP) is a high impact program focused on building an integrated community of public - private collaborative leaders. These leaders will be equipped with the skills, capabilities, and mindset required to accelerate the economic potential of Oman, promote its sustainable growth, and enhance its international competitiveness..

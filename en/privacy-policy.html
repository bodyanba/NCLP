Security & Privacy Policy

Security & Privacy Policy

We are committed to our obligation to protect the privacy rights of all our website visitors.

Information Collection & Usage

We do not collect any personal information about you, when you visit our web site, unless otherwise specified by you or you choose to provide this information to us through the application registration form.

However, upon your visit the following information are automatically collected and stored:

  • The IP address, from which you access the Internet
  • The date and time of your access to our website
  • The pages you visited (recorded by the text and graphics files that compose the page)

We use this information solely for statistical and monitoring purposes and to help us identify which services are most or least interesting to our visitors, as well as to monitor the website performance.

We may use third party consultants, tools or software for conducting statistical analysis; however please note that only certain pieces of aggregated, non-personal information, such as the number of users who accessed our website, or pages most visited etc are collected. Such information does not identify you individually. We keep all collected information confidential except where disclosure is enforced or required by law, or as part of requirement to protect our rights and intellectual properties.

Security of Transactions

We have implemented generally accepted standards of technology and operational security. Please be assured that this site has security measures in place to protect the information you provide us. We use encryption methods to protect all information, which is deemed to be sensitive, or any other data that should remain secure to meet the legal requirements.

Links to third-party websites

Our website may provide links to other web sites. However, we are not responsible for the following:

  • Accuracy of the contents of these other sites
  • Their mechanisms for information collection and usage
  • If you connect to any other site through our website, you need to read its Security & Privacy Policy to determine its practices related to information collection and usage.

Security & Privacy Policy Changes

We reserve the right to change this Security & Privacy Policy at any time. The amended version of the Security & Privacy Policy will be always published on this website.

Privacy Intrusion

If you doubt that your privacy is being compromised during the access of this website, do:

  • Close all tabs and windows you are browsing our website with.

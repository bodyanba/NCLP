Orientation Session

Muscat, Oman

The Orientation Session will focus on building a community of learners, providing orientation to the programme, giving participants an overview of the learning journey, an initial sense of the activities and challenges to come, and the resources at their disposal.

1 / The Engagement

Jebel Akhdar, Oman

This highly experiential and practical three-day module brings together participants from the Public and Private Sectors. Working in partnership with Henley Business School, the Module has been designed to start the process of building relationships across Sectors, to establish a common language and to identify those characteristics that make for great collaboration.

The Module will achieve this through a range of practical team activities, intended to facilitate communication and build trust. Participants will also have the opportunity to understand better their own personal leadership style and how they might address future opportunities and challenges from a leadership perspective. Finally, the Module will introduce the theme of competitiveness and will start the process of exploring those criteria which the Sultanate needs to address.

2 / The Immersion

Singapore

This module immerses participants in the world of 4IR (4th Industrial Revolution).

Working in partnership with Nanyang Technological University (NTU), the five days will be spent developing an understanding of the main dimensions of 4IR including Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, IoT and Connected Devices, Blockchain and Distributed Ledger, Autonomous and Urban Mobility, Digital Trade and Cross Border Data Flows.

Through a combination of expert speakers, panel discussions, immersion into the future world of work, and then practical application, participants will gain a good awareness of 4IR and the implications for the competitiveness of Oman, their organisations, and their leadership roles now and into the future. Edgar the Robot will be one of the Program Directors for the Module.
